
# Préci

Studying is broken.

Reading notes doesn’t work.
Highlighting things is fake productivity.
Rewatching lectures is just procrastination wearing glasses.

Préci exists to fix that.

This app turns your study material into a **high-intensity learning game** that forces your brain to actually learn things instead of pretending to.

Upload your documents.

Préci rips them apart, finds what matters, generates questions, and throws them back at you until the knowledge is burned into your brain.

No fluff.
No passive learning.
No pretending.

Just **pure recall training**.

The entire system is designed around real cognitive science principles like **active recall, the forgetting curve, and Pareto learning** so you spend time learning what actually matters. 

---

# The Philosophy

Brains are lazy.

If something feels like work, your brain avoids it.

So we did the obvious thing.

We made studying feel like a **game you don’t want to stop playing**.

Préci is built around three rules:

### 1. Force Recall

You don't learn by reading.

You learn by **trying to remember and failing**.

So the entire system revolves around answering questions.

Constantly.

---

### 2. Train the Forgetting Curve

Your brain forgets things on a timer.

Préci tracks that timer and brings questions back **right when your brain is about to forget them**.

Not earlier.
Not later.

Exactly when the memory needs reinforcement.

---

### 3. Stay in the Pain Zone

If something is too easy → you get bored.

If something is too hard → you quit.

The sweet spot is **~85% accuracy**.

That means:

* you’re getting things right
* you’re getting things wrong
* your brain is actively adapting

Préci constantly adjusts difficulty to keep you there.

Maximum learning.
Minimum burnout.

---

# What Happens When You Upload Notes

You upload a document.

Préci then:

1. **Analyzes the content**
2. **Identifies the most important concepts**
3. **Generates question stacks**
4. **Ranks them by importance**
5. **Starts you at easy questions**
6. **Pushes you toward harder ones**

Every session becomes a **progression system**.

You’re not just answering questions.

You’re **leveling up knowledge**.

---

# Game Modes

## Single Player

Pure learning mode.

You fight the question stack.

You get tracked on:

* accuracy
* speed
* improvement
* streaks

Progress charts show whether you’re actually getting better or just guessing.

---

## Dual Mode

1v1 knowledge battle.

Both players get the same question stack.

More correct answers wins.

No mercy.

---

## Multiplayer

Every question is a round.

Correct answer wins the round.

Most rounds won takes the crown.

---

# Streak System

Consistency builds mastery.

Préci tracks streaks across question stacks and topics.

Break the streak?

Your squid gets sad.

Keep the streak?

Happy squid.

Yes we weaponized a squid for motivation.

You’re welcome.

---

# Question Engine

Questions are not random.

They are built using two major systems:

### Pareto Learning

The system detects the **most repeated and important concepts** in your documents and prioritizes them first.

You master the high-impact material early.

---

### Progressive Difficulty

Questions move:

easy → medium → hard

Your performance determines how fast you climb.

---

# Progress Tracking

Préci measures three things:

**Accuracy**
How many you get right.

**Speed**
How fast you answer.

**Consistency**
How often you show up.

The app visualizes all of this with progress charts so improvement is obvious.

Watching those graphs climb is dangerously addictive.
